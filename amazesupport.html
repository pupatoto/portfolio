Amaze Support

This is a project I worked on during my internship at Gosoft I developed an iframe page for Amaze Application using Next.js and Node.js,
It displays general customer information, linking data between Amaze and other BU under CP Group, transaction history of point exchanges
between Amaze and BUs, point expiration details, and customer PDPA data.
